Teaching

Teaching is my favorite part of my appointment as a graduate student. I am passionate about finding ways to make Computer Science and Mathematics more accessible to students and the public.

I have had the privilege of serving as a TA for two courses so far: Artificial Intelligence 1 (CSCI 5511), and Elementary Computational Linear Algebra (CSCI 2033) in the Fall and Spring of AY 2017-2018 respectively.

I also serve as our lab's outreach coordinator, occasionally giving lab demos for student groups from local high schools and middle schools. If you are interested in organizing a demo, please feel free to send me an email.
