Research

My research interests are in the development and use of data-intensive computational methods such as deep learning to advance our understanding of cancer. A list of my publications can be found on Google Scholar.

ULabel Annotation Tool

I actively maintain ULabel, an open-source web-based tool for image annotation.

KiTS Challenge

I was the lead organizer of the 2019 Kidney and Kidney Tumor Segmentation Challenge (KiTS) associated with MICCAI 2019. You can find more information about this on the KiTS19 homepage.

We are in the process of organizing a second iteration of this challenge in conjunction with MICCAI 2021. More information can be found on the KiTS21 homepage.
